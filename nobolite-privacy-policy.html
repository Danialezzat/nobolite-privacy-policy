Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Nobolite collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the Nobolite mobile application, website, and related services. By using Nobolite, you agree to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you use Nobolite:

  • Account Information: your email address, login credentials, verification codes, and account status.
  • Profile Information: your display name, public handle or ID, bio, motivational sentence, profile image, account type, and region details such as country or state.
  • User Content: comments, replies, likes, follows, votes, reports, profile updates, and other content you choose to create or share.
  • Ranking and Activity Data: community pulse activity, follower and following relationships, ranking visibility settings, and public engagement statistics.
  • Technical Information: device and app usage information, IP address, general diagnostics, crash data, approximate region signals, and similar technical data necessary to operate and secure the service.
  • Uploaded Media: profile photos and other images you submit through the service.
  • Communications: password reset emails, OTP login or verification emails, service notices, and support messages.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • create and manage your Nobolite account;
  • authenticate sign-in attempts and send verification or password reset codes;
  • display and maintain your profile, profile image, rankings, and public reputation activity;
  • enable comments, replies, follows, likes, notifications, and community pulse interactions;
  • prevent spam, abuse, impersonation, fraud, unauthorized access, and manipulation of rankings or engagement features;
  • moderate content and enforce our community standards;
  • improve app performance, reliability, and user experience;
  • respond to support requests and account-related inquiries; and
  • comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Public Information and Visibility

Nobolite is designed around public reputation and profile visibility. Some information may be visible to other users by default, including your public handle, display name, profile image, public comments and replies, follower counts, and ranking-related signals.

You may have access to settings that control whether certain ranking details are visible, but even if rankings are hidden from public display, your account may still remain searchable within the service where appropriate for discovery and platform functionality.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following situations:

  • With other users: when your profile or content is intentionally public within the app.
  • With service providers: hosting, infrastructure, storage, database, analytics, authentication, email delivery, customer support, and similar operational providers that help us run Nobolite.
  • For legal reasons: if required to comply with law, protect user safety, investigate misuse, or enforce our rights and policies.
  • Business changes: if Nobolite is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset transfer, information may be transferred as part of that transaction subject to applicable law.

5. Email and Verification Services

Nobolite may send one-time passwords, login codes, password reset emails, security verification emails, and important account notices. These messages are required for account security and service functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you disable your account, some information may remain retained for operational, legal, safety, audit, or abuse-prevention reasons. If you request deletion of your account, we will process that request in accordance with our systems, legal obligations, and legitimate retention needs.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • access or review certain account information;
  • update your profile details and account settings;
  • request password reset access or security verification;
  • disable your account;
  • request deletion of your account; and
  • contact us regarding questions about your personal information.

To make a privacy-related request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards intended to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Nobolite is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service without parental permission. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, contact us and we will review the matter.

10. International Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, depending on where our systems or service providers operate. By using Nobolite, you understand that information may be transferred to and processed in these locations subject to applicable safeguards and legal requirements.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or operational improvements. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of Nobolite after an updated policy becomes effective means the updated policy will apply going forward.
