Child Safety Standards

Nobolite is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and to taking swift action against content, behavior, or accounts that threaten the safety of minors.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Nobolite prohibits any content, conduct, grooming behavior, solicitation, exploitation, trafficking, sexualization of minors, or any other material connected to child sexual abuse and exploitation. Any such activity is forbidden on the platform.

2. Prohibited Content and Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited:

  • content depicting or promoting child sexual abuse or exploitation;
  • sexualized comments, replies, messages, or profile content involving minors;
  • grooming, coercion, or predatory contact with minors;
  • sharing, requesting, linking to, or distributing exploitative material;
  • impersonation or deceptive behavior intended to target minors; and
  • any attempt to use Nobolite to facilitate abuse, exploitation, or trafficking.

3. Detection and Enforcement

Nobolite may review reports, account activity, uploaded content, profile information, comments, replies, and other relevant platform signals to detect abusive or exploitative behavior. Where appropriate, we may remove content, restrict visibility, suspend accounts, disable access, or permanently delete accounts.

4. Reporting and Escalation

Users may report safety concerns by contacting our support team. Nobolite reserves the right to investigate reported content or conduct and to escalate matters to relevant authorities, child-safety organizations, or legal processes where required.

5. Cooperation With Law Enforcement

Nobolite may preserve relevant records and cooperate with lawful requests from authorities where necessary to protect users, investigate abuse, and comply with applicable legal obligations related to child safety.

6. Account Action and Removal

Accounts found to be involved in CSAE-related activity may be removed immediately and permanently. Nobolite may also restrict, review, or remove related content or associated accounts where needed to protect the platform and its users.
